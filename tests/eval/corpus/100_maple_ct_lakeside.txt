Engineering Report - 100 Maple Court, Lakeside, FL 30001

Property Overview
The subject property is a two-story wood-framed residence with an attached garage and a gable roof. The site visit was conducted to evaluate storm-related damage reported by the homeowner.

Roof Observations
Several shingles were missing or creased on the windward slope. The damage created an opening through which wind-driven rain entered the roof assembly during the storm.

Interior Observations
In the garage, water damage was noted on the ceiling and the upper portion of the north wall. The staining and softened drywall are consistent with water entering through the storm-created opening above.

On the bedroom windowsill, cracking was observed. The cracking is attributable to water that entered through the storm-created opening and pooled at the sill.

Floor tile in the entry hall was no longer adhering to the slab in several locations. The lifting of the floor tiles is consistent with moisture intrusion following the storm.

Conclusion
The observed interior damage, including the lifted floor tiles and garage ceiling staining, is the result of water intrusion through a storm-created opening in the roof. The conditions are consistent with the reported windstorm and not with gradual deterioration.
