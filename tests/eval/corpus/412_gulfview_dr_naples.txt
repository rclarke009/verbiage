Engineering Report - 412 Gulfview Drive, Naples, FL 34102

Property Overview
The subject property is a single-story stucco residence with a tile roof. The inspection was requested to determine the cause of reported interior moisture.

Roof Observations
The roof tiles were intact and properly seated, with no displaced or missing tiles observed. No storm-created opening was identified in the roof covering during the inspection.

Plumbing Observations
A supply line under the kitchen sink exhibited an active drip. The cabinet base and adjacent flooring were saturated, and the staining radiated outward from the plumbing connection.

Conclusion
The interior moisture damage at this residence originates from a plumbing supply-line leak beneath the kitchen sink. The damage is unrelated to weather and is not attributable to a storm-created opening. We recommend repair of the supply line by a licensed plumber.
